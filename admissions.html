Form One Admissions

Admission to St. Mary's Kibabii (Kiba) is through the Ministry of Education's national placement system for National Schools.

  • KCPE cut-off: 400+ marks
  • National school placement
  • Selection from all 47 counties

Fee Structure

As a public National School, fees at Kiba are regulated by the Ministry of Education.

  • Annual fees: KES 45,000 - 65,000
  • Payment plans available
  • Bursaries for needy students
