Daily Prayers

Faith, Reflection, and Spiritual Growth at Kiba

Morning Prayer

Heavenly Father, as we begin this new day at St. Mary's Kibabii, we ask for Your guidance and wisdom. Bless our teachers, staff, and fellow students. Help us to learn, grow, and serve You in all we do. Amen.

Prayer Before Study

Come Holy Spirit, enlighten our minds and strengthen our memory. Help us to learn diligently and understand deeply what we study. May all our knowledge lead us closer to You. Amen.

Grace Before Meals

Bless us, O Lord, and these Your gifts which we are about to receive from Your bounty. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Evening Prayer

Lord, as we end this day, we thank You for Your blessings. Forgive us for any wrongs we have committed. Grant us peaceful rest and renew us for tomorrow. Amen.

Prayer to St. Mary

Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou among women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners, now and at the hour of our death. Amen.

Prayer for Students

Almighty God, pour out Your Spirit upon all students of Kiba. Grant them focus, determination, and perseverance. May they use their talents for Your glory. Amen.
