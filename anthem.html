St. Mary's Kibabii School Song

In the heart of Bungoma, where the hills stand tall,
Stands our beloved Kibabii, answering the call.
Guided by our motto, "Orare et Laborare",
We strive for excellence, in all we do and share.

Pray and work together, this our solemn creed,
Building men of character, planting wisdom's seed.
Faith and knowledge joined as one, under skies so blue,
St. Mary's Kibabii, we lift our praise to you.

Through the halls of learning, where our dreams take flight,
Guided by our teachers, towards the shining light.
Science, sports, and service, all our gifts employ,
Growing into leaders, spreading hope and joy.

When we leave these gates behind, to the world we go,
Carrying the values that our school did bestow.
Once a Kibabiian, always true and strong,
St. Mary's Kibabii, this our lifelong song.
