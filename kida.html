Once a Kibabiian, Always a Kibabiian

Kibabiians Development Association

KIDA is the official alumni association of St. Mary's Kibabii Boys National School (Kiba). Established to unite all former students (Kibabiians) across generations, KIDA serves as a bridge between the school and its alumni community worldwide.

Our Mission

To foster a lifelong connection among Kibabiians, support the development of St. Mary's Kibabii (Kiba), and create opportunities for mentorship and networking that benefit both current students and alumni.

What We Do

  • Organize annual homecoming reunions
  • Coordinate mentorship programs for current students
  • Fundraise for school infrastructure projects
  • Connect Kibabiians working across Kenya and globally
  • Celebrate alumni achievements through recognition awards
