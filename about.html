About Us

Our History, Mission, and Values

Founded in 1952 by the Mill Hill Fathers, St. Mary's Kibabii Boys National School (affectionately known as "Kiba") stands as a beacon of academic and moral excellence in Bungoma County. Promoted to National School status in 2025, we now admit students from all 47 counties of Kenya.

Our Latin motto "Orare et Laborare" (Pray and Work) shapes young men into disciplined leaders who serve God and society with integrity. We are sponsored by the Catholic Church and maintain strong Christian values while embracing academic innovation.
